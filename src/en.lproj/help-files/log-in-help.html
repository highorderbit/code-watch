Logging in will allow you to quickly view your personal news feed and user profile. Provide your API token (optional) to view your private repos and private news feed.

Username

Your exact, case-insensitive GitHub username.

Token

Your GitHub API token -- not your password. This will allow you to view private repos and your private news feed. Find your token on the "account" page on the GitHub website. Your token will be encrypted and stored in your keychain.
